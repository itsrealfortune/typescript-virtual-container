BASE64(1)                User Commands                   BASE64(1)

NAME
       base64 - encode/decode data in base64

SYNOPSIS
       base64 [OPTION]... [FILE]

OPTIONS
       -d, --decode    decode data
       -w, --wrap=COLS wrap encoded lines after COLS characters (default 76, 0=no wrap)

EXAMPLES
       echo 'hello' | base64
       echo 'aGVsbG8=' | base64 -d
